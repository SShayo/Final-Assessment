Our Hiring Process

Application

Have you found a job that matches your skills? Don’t hesitate and apply via the form - if you don't have a CV ready, feel free to use your LinkedIn profile. Our professional Recruiters will review and get back to you with the feedback.

Cultural Interview

We want to know what kind of person you are, and what are your values and aspirations at work and in life in general. Therefore, you will speak with our Recruiter and later with the Hiring Manager, who will tell you more about the role and responsibilities.

Practical assignment

We want to know more about your way of working by sending you a home takeaway assignment, which will be very relevant to the work you are going to do at Dot. Afterward, we might invite you for a follow-up interview.

Chat with Management Team

We want you to meet the people who run the organization, and they will have the final say in the process. You can expect a friendly chat about how the company was created and our goals and ask any questions left during the interview.

Decision

If you enjoyed the process and opportunity and we are enthusiastic as well, you can expect a job offer from us. Please note, that if the decision is different, we are committed to giving you constructive feedback in any case. Communication and relationships are very important for us!
